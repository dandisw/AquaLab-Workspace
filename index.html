📐 Panduan Formula & Referensi Ilmiah
1. Performa Budidaya (In Vivo)
SR (Survival Rate) = (Nt / N0) × 100 (%)
ADG (Average Daily Growth) = (Wt − W0) / t (g/hari)
SGR (Specific Growth Rate) = [(ln Wt − ln W0) / t] × 100 (%/hari)
FCR (Feed Conversion Ratio) = F / (Biomassa Akhir − Biomassa Awal)
EP (Efisiensi Pakan) = (Pertambahan Biomassa / F) × 100 (%)
Referensi: Lugert, V., Thaller, G., Tetens, J., Schulz, C., & Krieter, J. (2016). A review on fish growth calculation: multiple functions in common use, need for a standardized procedure. Reviews in Aquaculture, 8(1), 50-62. doi:10.1111/raq.12099
2. Farm Management (Bioflok & Dosimetri Kimia)
Kebutuhan Karbon Bioflok (\(\Delta C\)) = TAN (g) \(\times\) Target C/N Ratio. Dosis Molase (g) = \(\Delta C\) / (%C Bahan Baku / 100)
Kebutuhan Bahan Kimia (Sterilisasi) = (Volume Air \(\times\) Target ppm) / (% Kemurnian Aktif) / 1000.
Ref (Bioflok): Crab, R., Defoirdt, T., Bossier, P., & Verstraete, W. (2012). Biofloc technology in aquaculture: beneficial effects and future challenges. Aquaculture, 356, 351-356. doi:10.1016/j.aquaculture.2012.04.016
3. Reproduksi & Pembenihan (Gonad)
GSI (Gonadosomatic Index) = (Wg / Wt) × 100 (%)
HSI (Hepatosomatic Index) = (Wh / Wt) × 100 (%)
Fekunditas Gravimetri = (Wg / w) × n (butir telur)
Referensi: Brewer, S. K., Rabeni, C. F., & Papoulias, D. P. (2008). Reproductive biology of the Topeka shiner. Transactions of the American Fisheries Society, 137(3), 728-742. doi:10.1577/T07-074.1
Standar Nasional: SNI 8111:2015 (Pembenihan Ikan Nila).
4. Kesehatan Darah Ikan & Udang
Penghitungan Haemocytometer: Sel/mL = (Total Sel / Jml Kotak) \(\times\) 10⁴ \(\times\) Pengenceran.
Tingginya TPC di organ hati (> 4 log CFU/g) mengindikasikan infeksi bakteri sistemik. Peningkatan drastis Gula Darah mengindikasikan hiperglikemia akibat respons stres hormon kortisol.
Ref (Hematologi): Blaxhall, P. C., & Daisley, K. W. (1973). Routine haematological methods for use with fish blood. Journal of Fish Biology, 5(6), 771-781. doi:10.1111/j.1095-8649.1973.tb04510.x
Ref (Gula Darah): Barton, B. A. (2002). Stress in fishes: a diversity of responses. Integrative and Comparative Biology, 42(3), 517-525. doi:10.1093/icb/42.3.517
Ref (Hemolymph Udang): Johansson, M. W., Keyser, P., Sritunyalucksana, K., & Söderhäll, K. (2000). The haemocytes of crustaceans. Aquaculture, 191(1-3), 45-52. doi:10.1016/S0044-8486(00)00342-9
5. Nutrisi & Manajemen Pakan
NFE (Nitrogen-Free Extract) = 100 - (Protein + Lemak + Serat + Abu + Air)
Formulasi Pearson Square digunakan untuk mencari proporsi 2 bahan (Basal & Suplemen) terhadap target nutrisi.
Referensi: National Research Council (NRC). (2011). Nutrient Requirements of Fish and Shrimp. National Academies Press. doi:10.17226/13039
6. Kualitas Air & Plankton
Indeks Shannon-Wiener (H') = - \(\sum(P_i \ln P_i)\), di mana \(P_i\) adalah proporsi individu spesies ke-i.
NH₃ Bebas (Un-ionized Ammonia) = TAN × fraksi(pH, T°C). Batas letal: ≤ 0.02 mg/L.
Laju Pertumbuhan (K) = (ln N₂ − ln N₁) / (t₂ − t₁).
Ref (Ekologi): Shannon, C. E. (1948). A mathematical theory of communication. The Bell System Technical Journal, 27(3), 379-423. doi:10.1002/j.1538-7305.1948.tb01338.x
Ref (Amonia): Emerson, K., Russo, R. C., Lund, R. E., & Thurston, R. V. (1975). Journal of the Fisheries Board of Canada, 32(12), 2379-2383. doi:10.1139/f75-274
Ref (Plankton Kinetika): Hillebrand, H., et al. (1999). Biovolume calculation for pelagic and benthic microalgae. Journal of Phycology, 35(3), 403-424. doi:10.1046/j.1529-8817.1999.3520403.x
Standar Nasional: SNI 8228.1:2015 (Kualitas Air Udang Vannamei).
7. Lab In Vitro (Mikrobiologi & Molekuler)
Suhu Annealing PCR (Ta) = Tm terkecil dari primer − 5 °C.
Ref (Tm Primer): SantaLucia, J. (1998). A unified view of polymer, dumbbell, and oligonucleotide DNA nearest-neighbor thermodynamics. PNAS, 95(4), 1460-1465. doi:10.1073/pnas.95.4.1460
