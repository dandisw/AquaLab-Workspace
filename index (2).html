📐 Panduan Formula & Validasi
In Vivo — Performa Budidaya
SR = (Nt / N0) × 100 (%)
ADG = (Wt − W0) / t (g/hari)
SGR = [(ln Wt − ln W0) / t] × 100 (%/hari)
FCR = F / (Wt×Nt − W0×N0)
Ref: Lugert, V. et al. (2016). "A review on fish growth calculation...". Reviews in Aquaculture. doi:10.1111/raq.12099
EWS Kualitas Air — Toksisitas Amonia
NH₃ bebas = TAN × fraksi(pH, T°C)
dimana fraksi = 1 / (1 + 10^(pKa − pH)) dan pKa = 0.09018 + 2729.92 / (T+273.15).
Batas aman letal: ≤ 0.02 mg/L.
Ref: Emerson, K. et al. (1975). "Aqueous ammonia equilibrium calculations...". J. Fish. Res. Bd. Can. doi:10.1139/f75-274
TPC — Mikrobiologi
TPC = (Σ koloni / vol plating mL) × 10faktor pengenceran (CFU/mL).
Rentang valid statistik: 30–300 koloni per cawan. Di luar rentang → tandai TFTC/TNTC.
Ref: Sutton, S. (2011). "Accuracy of plate counts". Journal of Validation Technology, 17(3), 42-46.
Biokinetika — Kinetika Fase Log
Laju Spesifik (µ) = (ln OD₂ − ln OD₁) / (t₂ − t₁) (jam⁻¹)
Waktu Generasi (td) = ln(2) / µ = 0.693 / µ (jam)
Ref: Widdel, F. (2007). "Theory and measurement of bacterial growth". Grundpraktikum Mikrobiologie.
Zona Hambat — Uji Antagonis
Lebar zona hambatan asli = (Ø total − Ø sumuran) / 2 (mm)
Kategori Davis & Stout (1971): <5=Lemah · 5–10=Sedang · 10–20=Kuat · >20=Sangat Kuat.
Ref: Davis, W.W., & Stout, T.R. (1971). "Disc plate method...". Applied Microbiology. doi:10.1128/am.22.4.659-665.1971
Primer PCR — Kalkulasi Tm
Primer pendek (<14 nt): Tm = 2(A+T) + 4(G+C) °C
Primer panjang (≥14 nt): Tm = 64.9 + 41×(G+C−16.4) / panjang °C
Ta optimal = Tm terkecil − 5 °C.
Ref: SantaLucia, J. (1998). "A unified view of polymer, dumbbell, and oligonucleotide DNA nearest-neighbor thermodynamics". PNAS. doi:10.1073/pnas.95.4.1460
